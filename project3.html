The Build

What I Built

A breakdown of the core pieces that made this CRM work.

Custom Objects

Built six custom objects, including Customer Info, Travel Package, Booking, Booking Guest, Booking Payment, and Employee, connected through lookup relationships.

Automated Workflows

Used Salesforce Flows to automate booking confirmations, payment reminders, and client follow-ups, removing manual back-and-forth.

Validation & Security

Set up validation rules to keep data accurate, plus role-based access and field-level security to protect sensitive information like payment details.

Dashboards & Reports

Built dashboards and reports to visualize bookings, revenue, and travel agent performance for management.
